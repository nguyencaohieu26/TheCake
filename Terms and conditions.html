TERMS & CONDITIONS

Thank you for choosing TheCake

These terms and conditions serve as the contract between the customer and The Cake. By paying this invoice, the customer is agreeing to the these terms and conditions. Terms and conditions are subject to change without notice.

Delivery

Orders for postal delivery are currently despatched on Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday each week. Orders must be received by 12pm the day prior to delivery in order to leave time for freshly baking your items. Some items may require a longer turn-around. All our perishable products are sent to you fresh and securely packed to arrive the following day - as such we are able to deliver only to Zones A and B.

Payments

All sales are final.

All payments are non-refundable. Store credit can only be issued by The Cake Management. Any refund or store credit requests must be submitted in writing via email to info@thecake.com. The Cake has 14 days to review this request and determine an outcome. The outcome will be sent the email address of the requester.

Wedding orders

When ordering 14 days or more in advance, a 50% non-transferable deposit in required to confirm your reservation. Final payment is due fourteen (14) days prior to event date.

If this order has been placed within 14 days of the event date, payment in full is required to confirm your reservation. If the event is postponed for any reason, please submit the new event date no later than 75 days prior to the event.

Occasional cake orders (birthday, shower, anniversary, etc.)

When ordering 14 days or more in advance, a 50% non-transferable deposit in required to confirm your reservation. Final payment is due seven (7) days before delivery or pickup of the items ordered. If this order has been placed within 14 days of the event date, payment in full is required to confirm your reservation. A 15% late fee will be charged each day the final payment is late.

Sole provider

For reputation-management and food-safety reasons, all cakes or desserts for your event must be supplied by The Cake. If any cake is provided by an individual or company other than The Cake without prior notification and our express written consent, we reserve the right to leave your venue without setting up your cake, and all payments made will be forfeited. The undelivered items will be disposed of at The Cake's discretion.

Changes

The final date of any changes for the size, flavor, style, etc. of the cake are due 30 days prior to the event. Any changes after this date cannot be guaranteed and may be subject to additional charges. All changes to existing orders must be submitted in writing through email only. Seasonal items may incur an additional charge or may be substituted if unavailable.

Variations in design

While The Cake makes every effort to provide exactly the flavor, size, shape, and design discussed during consultations, creating cake is an art form and may be subject to variations.

In the event that the product requires equipment or ingredients that cannot be obtained within the time allotted, the closest resemblance available will be used to complete the product.

Site & Venue conditions

The Cake reserves the right to refuse set up of a cake in any location that is not properly air conditioned, or on any cake plateau/stand that does not appear sturdy enough to hold the weight of our cakes.

The Cake is not responsible for any damage caused by tables or cake plateaus/stands that were unable to withstand the weight of our cakes or improperly installed.

Rights & Waivers

  • The Cake is not responsible for any damage to the cake after delivery/set up is complete.
  • If the customer elects to pick up the cake, the customer is responsible for the cake as soon as it leaves The Cake's premises.
  • The product may contain milk, nuts, and other allergens. The customer is responsible for notifying the guests. The Cake is not responsible for any adverse effects of allergic reactions.

Equipment rental

All equipment rental prices include a rental fee and a deposit. If the rented items are returned to The Cake within 3 days, the deposit will be returned to the customer electronically If the item is not returned within 3 days, the customer forfeits the deposit. If shipping the items back to The Cake, the customer will have 5 business days and must provide a shipping tracking number via email when shipped.

Cancellations

Any cancellations must be made 72 hours in advance in writing and will result in a Store Credit ONLY.
