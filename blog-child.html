6 New Cakes For Every Occasion

6 New Cakes For Every Occasion

17th September, 2020. / Our Food

Every cake tells a story. Here are some new cakes from us to help you celebrate your stories.

Birthdays, anniversaries, christenings, retirements, passing exams, getting new jobs ... all occasions to be marked as special milestones and definitely worthy of cake delivery!

Every cake tells a story, even if it’s just coffee and a cake as a break from work, or a cheerful family afternoon tea. A cake can be an essential part of a tradition of family sharing, or the centrepiece of a gathering to celebrate a happy event.

Whatever the story, PAUL has a cake, or a dessert, to make it special.

These are the NEW cakes joining our range, and they are available for click & collect or delivery via our website! Order online before 12 noon for next day delivery:

Celebration Carrot Cake: everyone loves a carrot cake, and we’ve created a splendid new 3-tiered carrot cake that’s perfect for special occasions. Serves 6 – 8.

Small Chocolate Macaron Cake: our Chocolate Macaron Cake is a best seller after only a few weeks, and this is its baby brother. Serving 6 - 8 (rather than 10 – 12) it’s just as decadent. Layers of chocolate sponge, filled with strawberry jam and chocolate butter cream; smothered in more butter cream, topped with a dripping chocolate glaze and decorated with chocolate meringue rosettes, chocolate and caramel mini macarons, gold sugar balls and curls of gold leaf.

Victoria Sponge Cake: another perennial favourite of the tea table, given a taste of France - with a filling of PAUL Strawberry Jam and Chantilly Cream. Serves 4 – 6.

Cherry Clafoutis: when you’re looking for something different for dessert, try this French classic. A creamy egg custard enriched with ground almonds, filled with Morello cherries, topped with flaked almonds and baked. It’s particularly good served warm with a dollop of cream or crème fraîche. Serves 6.

Mixed Berry Swirl Cheesecake: another pretty dessert! Hidden filling of coulis made with strawberry, blackberry, morello cherry and redcurrant; with more swirls of coulis on top. Serves 6 – 8.

Blackcurrant Cheesecake: This blackcurrant cheesecake is a combination of rich, creamy cheesecake on a base of buttery, speculoos biscuit crumbs, given a stylish finish with blackcurrants & blackcurrant jam. Serve it as a dessert or enjoy as a treat any time. Serves 6 – 8.

All the cakes on our website are available for next day delivery, as long as you order before noon the day before. We hope you enjoy creating your own new stories with them.
